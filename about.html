Suzi Wine


I am a trained Coach with the Animas School of Transformative Coaching and a Member of the International Coaching Federation (ICF). I work according to the ICF Code of Ethics, details of which can be found on the ICF website. I have completed many hours of training and coached clients from young adults to those going through midlife changes and challenges.

Personal Trainer


As a qualified Personal Trainer I have used my knowledge of functional training, exercise and nutrition to inform my work as a coach in areas of self esteem, health and lifestyle.
