Get in touch

Suzi Wine

Health, Well Being & Lifestyle Coach

Hi I’m Suzi Wine and I work with people who are trying to work through life's challenges. People like you, who have goals they want to achieve, lifestyle balance they need to address or relationships they want to work through.


People who want to live a life that addresses the delicate balance of your own needs and those around us whilst staying in alignment with your values


As a Coach I am here to listen to you, help you work out a strategy and mindset that you can easily and comfortably integrate into your life in a transformative way. One that ultimately feels authentic and natural.

“Seek out that particular mental attribute which makes you feel most deeply and vitally alive, along with which comes the inner voice which says, ‘This is the real me,’ and when you have found that attitude, follow it.”
- William James


“When someone really hears you without passing judgment on you, without trying to take responsibility for you, without trying to mould you, it feels damn good…”
- Carl Rogers

My goal as a coach


MY goal as a Coach is to be here to serve my client - that's YOU.

My job is for you to feel the value and clarity that comes when you realise your own strength and confidence. I use an approach that provides you with the space to talk, fully explore your emotions and experiences and perhaps end up challenging some of your own limiting beliefs.

Through my initial six week package I look to help you create a lasting change that leaves you feeling accomplished, energised and positive about your future.

Coaching you to make Transformative Change with…

  • Health, Nutrition & Wellbeing
  • Motivation, Low Mood & Anxiety
  • Midlife Change & Uncertainty
  • Lack of Meaning & Purpose
  • Empty Nesting & Rediscovering Self
  • Relationships
  • Children, teens & parenting
