Do you sometimes look around you and ask yourself “How did I get here?”, “What am I doing”, “Why do I feel this way?”

As we journey through life, we change and develop, our work and family dynamic is constantly evolving. We are not the same person we were 10 or 20 years ago. Coaching can help you navigate this with a deeper understanding of yourself and help you make conscious choices for how you would best like to live your life.

As a Coach I see how being deeply listened to and heard can help you tap into your own inner resources, regain your sense of self and in turn reinstill a confidence and purpose that you may feel you have lost.

“Seek out that particular mental attribute which makes you feel most deeply and vitally alive, along with which comes the inner voice which says, ‘This is the real me,’ and when you have found that attitude, follow it.”
- William James


“When someone really hears you without passing judgment on you, without trying to take responsibility for you, without trying to mould you, it feels damn good…”
- Carl Rogers

My style of coaching


My goal as a Coach is to help you make a lasting and transformative change.
I use a non-directional approach, providing you with the space to talk and explore your values, emotions and experiences.
We tend to start with the present, look to where or what you wish for and how best to get there.
We use various modalities and techniques, with your permission, depending on what suits you.

Coaching you to make Transformative Change with…

  • Relationships
  • Children/Teens
  • Midlife Change & Uncertainty
  • Career Change and Decisions
  • Motivation, Low Mood & Anxiety
  • Health, Nutrition & Wellbeing
  • Lack of Meaning & Purpose
  • Empty Nesting & Rediscovering Self
