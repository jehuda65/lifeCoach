Get in touch

Call me to find out more

This is a chance for us to meet and for you to broach what is on your mind. It is a casual conversation, without any obligation to take it any further and completely free of charge. Just a chance to connect and for you to get a feel for what I can do for you.


The call will take between 20-30 minutes with no fee and no obligation but I will provide you with all the information you would want if you decide I am the right fit for you.

Packages

  • 6 Session Package

    • This is designed to take you from where you are now to where you would like to be. The focus is future orientated, listening to the challenges you may be facing and reflecting on what you have within you to purposefully transform the issue.

  • Workshop

    • This is useful when you have something more immediate to talk through. It follows on from previous work done in your 6 sessions and can be useful for specific, related, unforseen issues that may arise as a result. These typically take 90 minutes.

Agreement & questionnaire

Before we agree to the sessions I will provide a client / coach agreement which exists to safeguard both the client and the coach and enable you to see how your needs are important and I will hold boundaries for you.

Click HERE to download the agreement form


I will also send you a client questionnaire which will help me understand a little bit more about what you hope to get out of the coaching and will clarify certain aspects of the coaching experience for you too.
